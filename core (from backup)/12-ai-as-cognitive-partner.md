# AI as Cognitive Partner

AI does not replace thinking.

It amplifies it.

In VIRON, AI operates inside the Notes layer — not as a separate intelligence system.

It augments reasoning.
It does not generate structure.

---

## The Role of AI

AI may assist in:

- Clarifying language
- Refining summaries
- Generating alternative perspectives
- Extracting principles

But AI does not:

- Create projects
- Manage tasks
- Assign priorities
- Replace Weekly Review

AI operates inside the thinking boundary.

Separation remains intact.

## Guardrails

AI must not bypass:

- The Inbox
- Project structure
- Execution discipline
- Library refinement

It serves cognition.
It does not govern it.

---

## Architectural Principle

AI is a cognitive tool within Notes.

It is not an operational layer.

When AI remains inside the thinking boundary,
the architecture remains stable.

When AI crosses boundaries,
clarity erodes.

---

AI extends thought.

Structure preserves thought.

Both are necessary.

But only one governs.
