# Preface — The Friction Before the Framework

## Purpose of This Chapter

This Preface establishes the personal origin of VIRON.
It explains why the method was built, what problems preceded it,
and why structure became necessary rather than optional.

This is not autobiography.
It is narrative compression.

---

## Narrative Arc Overview

Competence → Frustration → Pattern Recognition → Constraint → Emergence → Naming → Purpose

---

## 1. Opening Scene — Competent, But Fragmented

**Paragraph Beat 1:**  
Establish professional competence. You were productive, capable, shipping work, managing responsibility.

**Paragraph Beat 2:**  
Introduce internal friction. Despite external output, systems felt unstable.

**Paragraph Beat 3:**  
Describe subtle disorder:
- Too many task systems
- Switching apps
- Growing backlogs
- Projects started faster than completed

Tone: Reflective, calm, analytical.

---

## 2. The Pattern Recognition Phase

**Paragraph Beat 4:**  
Describe discovering productivity systems with enthusiasm.

**Paragraph Beat 5:**

**Paragraph Beat 6:**  
Show the cycle:
Discovery → Setup → Short-term clarity → Drift → Abandonment

**Paragraph Beat 7:**  
State realization:
The problem was not discipline. It was structural instability.

**Paragraph Beat 10:**

- Notes

**Paragraph Beat 11:**
