# Diagrams & Mockups Registry

This registry tracks all diagrams, mockups, screenshots, and figure assets used in *The VIRON Method*. Its purpose is to provide a single source of truth for asset naming, chapter placement, captions, and editorial notes during drafting, design, layout, and export.

## Conventions

* **Asset ID** follows the format `CH##-MU##`
* **Type** distinguishes between conceptual diagrams, UI mockups, screenshots, and process visuals
* **Status** should be updated as the project advances through draft, design, and production
* **Path** assumes final image assets live in `/assets/`
* **Caption** should match the manuscript exactly where one has been defined

## Figure Numbering Note

Several later-chapter figures are still marked in the manuscript as **Figure X**. This registry preserves those captions as currently specified, but figure numbering should be normalized during final production after the full manuscript order is locked.
